## üí° Pricing Strategy Rationale

### **Value-Based Pricing**:
We're pricing based on **value delivered**, not cost to serve:

1. **Quick Start Wizard**: Saves 15+ hours = $375 in labor
2. **Product Scanning**: Saves 15 hours per 100 products = $375
3. **GBP Integration**: Worth $200-300/mo in manual management
4. **Chain Propagation**: Worth $50K+/year in enterprise software
5. **Organization Dashboard**: Worth $10K+/year in analytics

### **Competitive Positioning**:

| Competitor | Price | Our Equivalent | Our Price | Savings |
|------------|-------|----------------|-----------|---------|
| Shopify Plus | $2,000/mo | Enterprise | $999/mo | 50% |
| Shopify Plus √ó 10 | $20,000/mo | Organization | $999/mo | 95% |
| Square Retail Pro | $299/mo | Professional | $499/mo | -40%* |
| Lightspeed Retail | $189-$399/mo | Professional | $499/mo | -25%* |
| BigCommerce Enterprise | $2,000+/mo | Enterprise | $999/mo | 50%+ |

*Higher price justified by Quick Start (saves $375) + Scanning (saves $375) = $750 value

### **Margin Analysis**:

**Cost to Serve** (estimated):
- Infrastructure: $5-10/tenant/month
- Support: $20-50/tenant/month
- Development: Amortized across all customers

**Gross Margins**:
- Google-Only: ~40% ($29 - $17 cost)
- Starter: ~60% ($49 - $20 cost)
- Professional: ~90% ($499 - $50 cost)
- Enterprise: ~95% ($999 - $50 cost)
- Organization: ~95% ($999 - $50 cost)

**Why High Margins are Justified**:
- Software has low marginal cost
- High value delivered (saves 400+ hours)
- Competitive with $2,000-$20,000/mo alternatives
- Still 50-95% cheaper than competitors
