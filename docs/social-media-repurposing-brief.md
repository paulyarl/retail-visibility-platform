## Platform-by-Platform Strategy

### 1. LinkedIn (Primary B2B Channel)

**Native aspect ratio**: 1.91:1 (recommended), 1:1 (acceptable)
**Current fit**: The 16:9 images (~1.78:1) are close to native. Minor cropping or letterboxing recommended.

**Content strategy**:
- **Monday**: "The Mission" — Step 01 image with mission statement copy. Position as a thought leadership opener.
- **Tuesday–Thursday**: "Tier Deep-Dives" — One tier per day (Steps 02–07). Pair image with a specific merchant pain point and how this tier solves it.
- **Friday**: "Get Started" — Step 08 or mission image with CTA to the signup wizard.

**Sample post formula (Step 02 — Discovery)**:
```
Headline: Your products are already on Google. You just can't see them.

Body: Every time someone searches for something you sell, Google tries to show them where to find it. If your inventory isn't connected in real time, Google shows your competitor instead.

Discovery tier fixes that. Real-time Clover sync. No spreadsheets. No manual uploads.

[Image: step-02-discovery.png]

#RetailTech #LocalRetail #GoogleShopping #CloverPOS
```

**Hashtag set** (rotate):
- Primary: `#RetailTech` `#LocalRetail` `#SmallBusiness` `#InventoryManagement`
- Secondary: `#CloverPOS` `#GoogleShopping` `#ShopLocal` `#RetailInnovation`
- Tertiary: `#Omnichannel` `#Ecommerce` `#BrickAndMortar` `#POSIntegration`

### 2. Twitter/X (Thought Leadership + Quick Takes)

**Native aspect ratio**: 16:9 (ideal), 2:1 (acceptable)
**Current fit**: Excellent — images are already ~16:9.

**Content strategy**:
- **Thread starters**: Use mission image (Step 01) to anchor a 5-tweet thread on a single concept (e.g., "Why deposits beat abandoned carts for physical retailers").
- **Single tweets**: Use tier-specific images with one sharp insight. Keep under 280 chars.
- **Polls**: Pair a tier image with a poll — "Which describes your store? A) Discovery only B) Storefront C) Full e-commerce D) Omnichannel"

**Sample tweet (Step 04 — Commitment)**:
```
Physical retailers: You're losing money to "maybe I'll come in later."

A 10% deposit guarantees the visit. No abandoned inventory. No wasted prep time.

That's not friction — that's commitment.

[Image: step-04-commitment.png]
```

### 3. Instagram Feed (Visual Storytelling)

**Native aspect ratio**: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait)
**Current fit**: Landscape images need cropping or canvas expansion.

**Repurposing approach**:
- **Canvas method** (recommended): Place the 16:9 image on a 1:1 or 4:5 canvas. Add a solid background bar (brand teal `#0D9488` or coral `#F97316`) at top and bottom. Use the extra space for the tier name and one-line value prop.
- **Carousel posts**: Create a 7-slide carousel using all step images. Each slide shows one tier. Final slide is the "Get Started" CTA with the signup URL.
- **Caption formula**: Hook (pain point) → Insight (what changes) → CTA (link in bio).

**Sample carousel caption**:
```
Most retail platforms were built for online-only stores.

We built Visible Shelf for the other 90% — physical retailers who need shoppers to walk through the door.

Swipe to see how each tier moves you from "found on Google" to "closed at the register." 👉

Link in bio to start your free trial.

#LocalRetail #RetailTech #CloverPOS #ShopLocal
```

---

### 4. Instagram Stories / Reels (9:16 Vertical)

**Native aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080 × 1920)
**Current fit**: Significant reformatting needed.

**Repurposing approach**:
- **Split-screen Stories**: Use the top 60% for the artwork, bottom 40% for text overlay with the tier headline and one-line description.
- **Reel transitions**: Use the 7 step images as quick-transition slides (2 seconds each) with trending audio, ending on the "Get Started" image with a "Link in bio" sticker.
- **Text overlay style**: White or brand-coral text with a subtle dark scrim behind it for readability.

---

### 5. Facebook (Community + Groups)

**Native aspect ratio**: 1.91:1 (shared), 1:1 (recommended for engagement)
**Current fit**: Similar to LinkedIn — 16:9 works, 1:1 with canvas works better.

**Content strategy**:
- **Business Page**: Cross-post LinkedIn content. Facebook audiences respond well to "how it works" explainers.
- **Local Business Groups**: Share the Discovery and Storefront tier posts in local merchant/community groups. Lead with the local angle: "Get your inventory visible to shoppers already searching in [City]."
- **Events**: Use Enterprise tier image for webinar/event announcements targeting multi-location retailers.

## Design Guidelines for Repurposing

### Color palette (from image brief)
- **Primary**: `#0D9488` (teal)
- **Secondary**: `#0284C7` (blue)
- **Accent**: `#F97316` (coral)
- **Neutral**: `#F5F5F4` (warm gray)
- **Text**: `#171717` (near-black)

### Typography (if adding text overlays)
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif, 32–48pt
- Body: Regular sans-serif, 18–24pt
- Always maintain 4:5+ contrast ratio for accessibility

### Safe zones
- **Instagram 1:1 crop**: Center the focal point. Keep text/logo away from edges (min 60px padding).
- **Instagram 4:5 crop**: Same as above. Top and bottom will be cropped from the original 16:9.
- **Stories/Reels 9:16**: Place artwork in the upper 60%. Reserve lower 40% for text, CTAs, and stickers.
