## Phase 6: Why NOT Door-to-Door

### **The Math Doesn't Work**

**Door-to-Door Economics:**
```
Time per visit: 2-3 hours (travel + pitch)
Visits per day: 3-4
Conversion rate: 5-10%
Customers per day: 0.3
Customers per month: 6 (20 working days)
Cost per rep: $4,000/mo (salary + expenses)
CAC: $667 per customer

vs Digital Marketing:
Google Ads CAC: $143
Clover Marketplace CAC: $5
Content Marketing CAC: $0
```

**Scalability:**
```
Door-to-Door:
- 1 rep = 6 customers/mo
- 10 reps = 60 customers/mo
- Cost: $40,000/mo
- Not scalable beyond local area

Digital Marketing:
- $40,000/mo budget = 280 customers/mo
- Scales infinitely
- Works 24/7
- Global reach
```

---

### **When Door-to-Door MIGHT Make Sense**

**Only in these scenarios:**
1. **Enterprise/Organization tier** ($999/mo)
   - High contract value justifies in-person
   - Complex needs require consultation
   - Multi-location chains need hand-holding

2. **Strategic partnerships**
   - Meeting with Clover resellers
   - Presenting to business associations
   - Building relationships with key partners

3. **Local market validation** (first 10 customers)
   - Test messaging
   - Gather feedback
   - Refine product
   - Then switch to digital

**But NOT for:**
- ‚ùå Google-Only tier ($29/mo) - too low value
- ‚ùå Starter tier ($49/mo) - too low value
- ‚ùå Mass market acquisition - doesn't scale
